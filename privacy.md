# Privacy Policy – Multiply Master

_Last updated: January 2026_

Multiply Master respects your privacy. This application is designed to be fully usable without collecting or sharing any personal data.

## Data Collection

Multiply Master does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal information or user data.

- No account creation
- No analytics
- No advertising
- No tracking
- No internet connection required

## Local Data Storage

The app stores the following data **locally on your device only**:

- Game progress
- High scores
- App preferences

This data never leaves your device and is not shared with the developer or third parties.

## Internet Usage

Multiply Master does not require an internet connection to function.  
The app does not communicate with external servers.

## Third-Party Services

Multiply Master does not use any third-party services that collect user data, such as analytics providers, advertising networks, or tracking tools.

## Children’s Privacy

Multiply Master is suitable for children and does not knowingly collect any personal information from users of any age.

## Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes in the future, the updated version will be published at this URL.

## Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you can contact the developer via GitHub:

https://github.com/leensen
